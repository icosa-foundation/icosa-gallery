Icosa Gallery is open source software that allows you to store, share and use 3D models you've created in apps like Open Brush, Open Blocks, or anywhere else.

The main site can be found at icosa.gallery and you can host your own instance publicly or privately on premise for your own organisation to use. The code is available on GitHub.

We are currently working to make it easier to install and run, and we're in the process of improving our documentation. Feel free to contact us at contact@icosa.gallery.
